    How many shares are availeble?

    The value of one share is 1000 Birr, the minimum a person can purchase are 10 shares which in value are 10,000 Birr. And the maximum number one person can buy are 250,000 shares which in value are 250 Million Birr. The service charge is 5% of the subscriped share.

    What are the ways of payment?

    There are two ways of payment, one is you can pay 100% of your subscriped share amouts. The second method is you can pay half of your pledged Subscription amount and pay the rest within 6 months of the banks establishment. The service charge will be calculated from the Subscription amount. And service charge is none refunable.

    Can I pay in all currencies?

    No. Ethiopian citizens can purchase share in ETB but Diasporas are only eligible to buy shares with three currencies(Pound,Dollar or Euros). Accounting to NBE regulations.

    What are the necessary documents needed to buy shares?

    Ethiopian citizens can purchase shares by presenting an ID, driver's license, or passport. Ethiopians who have changed citizenship (diasporas) must present a passport and a yellow card (that identifies you are Ethiopian born). According to NBE regulations, someone who changed citizenship is only eligible to acquire shares if they can provide a card proving they were born in Ethiopia.
